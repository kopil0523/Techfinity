About Us

Techfinity Limited is a leading indigenous Information Communications Technology (ICT) company dedicated to delivering effective solutions tailored to the needs of the Banking, Financial Services, and Insurance (BFSI) sector, corporate offices, and government establishments.

Founded in November 2014, Techfinity began with a strong focus on software development, network installation, and the supply and consultation of software and hardware across various fields utilizing cutting-edge information technology. In April 2023, we officially registered as a limited company, reinforcing our commitment to providing quality-oriented products and services to every client we serve. Our youthful passion and dedication to innovation position us for long-term success in service provision.

As we continue to advance at a remarkable pace, our team of skilled and experienced professionals plays a crucial role in driving our growth and effectiveness. At Techfinity Limited, we operate on the core values of trust, integrity, and teamwork, which underpin our success and guide our everyday operations.

Our mission is to deliver fully tested products and solutions that consistently uphold the highest standards of quality and reliability for our customers. We are committed to establishing ourselves as a trusted leader in the ICT and communications industry by providing exceptional service and innovative solutions tailored to enhance our clients' operations. We strive to empower our customers through technology, ensuring that our offerings not only meet their immediate needs but also support their long-term success and growth. By fostering strong partnerships and maintaining a relentless focus on excellence, we aim to drive positive outcomes for our clients and contribute to the advancement of the industry as a whole.

Our vision is to consistently exceed our clients' expectations through exceptional service delivery and innovative product development, cultivating high levels of customer satisfaction and loyalty. We aspire to be the foremost technology enabler, empowering our customers to navigate their unique challenges and build successful futures through tailored solutions.

Moreover, we are dedicated to establishing Techfinity Limited as one of the most reputable and distinguished ICT companies in Bangladesh. We aim to achieve this by being recognized for our unwavering commitment to excellence, integrity, and innovation in every facet of our operations. Our focus on continuous improvement and adaptation to emerging technologies ensures that we remain at the forefront of the industry, providing our clients with cutting-edge solutions that drive sustainable growth and success. Through our collaborative approach and deep understanding of our clients' needs, we strive to be a trusted partner in their journey toward success.

Our Services

ICT Consultancy

We provide comprehensive ICT consultancy services that encompass:

  • Strategic Planning: Crafting tailored strategies that align with your business goals.
  • Assessment: Conducting thorough evaluations of existing systems to identify areas for improvement.
  • Procurement: Guiding the acquisition of the right technologies to enhance operational efficiency.
  • Re-engineering Solutions: Redesigning processes and systems to optimize performance and adaptability.
  • Planning, Audits, and Best Practices: Implementing industry best practices to ensure compliance and excellence.

Software Development

  • Application Development: Creating custom applications tailored to your specific requirements.
  • Application Maintenance: Ensuring ongoing support and updates to keep applications running smoothly.
  • Application Support: Providing technical assistance to resolve any issues promptly.
  • Application Integration/Migration/Transformation: Seamlessly integrating new applications with existing systems.
  • Application Management: Overseeing the entire application lifecycle to maximize value and performance.

Network & Infrastructure Services

We leverage IT investments through:

  • Minimizing Risks: Implementing robust security measures to protect your data and infrastructure.
  • Maximizing Compatibility: Ensuring that all systems and applications work harmoniously together.
  • Maximizing Interoperability: Facilitating seamless communication and data exchange between different systems.

Hardware Sales & Support Services

We offer a range of hardware solutions and support services, including:

  • Data Centre and Structured Cabling Systems: Designing and implementing efficient data centers and cabling infrastructures.
  • CISCO Equipment: Providing sales and support for switches, firewalls, wireless devices, and routers.
  • PC, Server, and Storage Solutions: Supplying and maintaining high-quality personal computers, servers, and storage systems.
  • Server and Switch Equipment Maintenance: Ensuring the reliability and longevity of your hardware through comprehensive maintenance services.
